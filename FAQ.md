# FAQ

## General Questions

### What is this bot?

AI Trading Signals Bot uses machine learning (LSTM) and technical analysis to generate trading signals for cryptocurrencies.

### Is this financial advice?

No! This is for educational purposes only. Always do your own research.

### Do I need programming knowledge?

Basic Python knowledge helps, but the bot is designed to be easy to use.

## Technical Questions

### What data sources are supported?

Currently supports Binance and Hyperliquid.

### Can I backtest strategies?

Yes! Use the backtest engine in `src/backtest.py`.

### How accurate are the predictions?

Accuracy varies by market conditions. Always use risk management.
